About Us

Who We Are Link to heading

RedRoom is a cyber security focused student society in ECU, where our focus is on learning and developing our skills. We are run by a group of passionate students who try to share their knowledge with their fellow students.

What We Do Link to heading

We participate in Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, run workshops on various computer science and cyber security topics, and have weekly meet up sessions, where you can socialise with others, or participate in some of our monthly CTF-based activities.

What is a CTF? Link to heading

CTF stands for Capture the Flag, but not the physical kind. CTFs are usually competitions that require solving cyber security puzzles (by “capturing the flag”) to gain points.

Want to Join? Link to heading

Great! We are always welcome to having more people joining us! Whether you are a noob at CTF, or a flag enthusiast, this is the place for you!
